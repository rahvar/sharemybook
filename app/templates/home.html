ABOUT US

Sharemybook runs on a crowd sourced platform model where hosts with libraries, rent, share and connect with passionate readers


Unlike traditional libraries or e-rental services,sharemybook scales not by scaling inventory but by increasing the variety of content and users with similar interests in books.

HOW IT WORKS

New incoming guest can register at the site. The User profile will mainlyly contain common details such as name , location etc. The user can put up a short bio and can also select his topic of Interests. After registrtation , an agent will physically document the books/library and a virtual Book cards will be uploaded to user profile. New registered users can then schedule visit to home libraries or rent books as displayed on the Host page.
